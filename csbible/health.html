NaturalHypertrophy

its completely fine if you cant lift heavy weights now, i couldnt lift heavy at all (on god i couldnt bench the bar bro i had to get the bro to pick that shit up off me) but over the course of a year i got to a 200 pound bench and a 300 pound deadlift naturally, its all about consistency thats all there is to it, diet plays a large role in the muscle synthesis too but you dont really need to make massive changes to the way you eat unless your like boogie2988 or nikocado, if you just eat a little more than you should adding exercise to your life will still see you lose weight overtime.

Diet

diet plays a large role in your fitness journey as well as proper nutrient intake, theres a pretty good chance you are nutrient starved and i really do think correcting that can lead to better performance in game and in your raw aim too. Look into cashing out on some multivitamins, magnesium, iron, zinc, vitamin a b c and fish oils, proper hydration goes a long way too in regards to general functionality and well being.

im not gonna get into crazy detail about diet just find out your BMR and adjust 500 > or < depending on the goal, if you want to gain weight > if you want to lose weight < and just remain in that surplus or deficit for how ever long it takes to hit the goal weight.
